
* PWM (Pulse Width Modulation) frequency
	* F_PWM = **F_CLK / (ARR+1)(PSC+1)**
	* ARR: Auto-Reload Register
	* PSC: Prescaler 預除頻值
	* RCC: Reset and Clock Control
* Duty Cycle
	* **CCRx / ARRx**
	* CCR: Capture Control Register
* Watchdog (WDG)
	* 發生錯誤時自動重置
	* 不斷往下數，到一個值之前都沒有refresh的話就認定系統出問題，觸發系統重置
